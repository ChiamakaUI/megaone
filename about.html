About Us

Overview

MegaOne Cinemas is an ultra-modern multiple screens movie exhibitor owned by Prime Mall Entertainment Centre. With four luxury state-of-the-art movie theatres located along Dreamworld Africana Way, by Lekki Conservation, Lekki Peninsular.

MegaOne Cinemas officially opened for business on the 31st October 2019 with the articulated intent to transform the movie experience in Lagos and offer the esteemed movie viewers an entirely fresh and unique movie experience.

At MegaOne Cinemas, all our theatres are fully equipped with the All-new Smart Laser Cinema Projectors. The laser technology delivers exceptional image quality with outstanding 2D & 3D motion pictures. By this, we pride our self as the first cinema in West Africa to introduce the laser technology in all our theatres. Our theatres also feature the most impressive mega-silver screens, with immersive 7.1 multidimensional surround sound technology in the comfiest seats around.

MegaOne Cinemas strives to offer clients an exceptional and unmatched cinema experience through promotions and activations; pre-release screenings combined with the highest level of personalized service and the very best in sound and projection technology.

We have everything and are strategically positioned to better your experience and make the MOVIE MAGIC COME LIFE. Come and experience MegaOne Cinemas.
